## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A valid email address
- Access to your email inbox for verification
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari (or the Petsgram mobile app)

<Callout kind="info">

Petsgram works on desktop and mobile. No special software required.

</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up and get verified.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Petsgram" icon="globe">

    Open your browser and go to [https://petsgram.net](https://petsgram.net).

  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">

    Click **Sign Up** in the top right.

    Fill in your details:

    - Email address
    - Password (at least 8 characters)
    - Pet name (e.g., "Buddy")

    Agree to terms and click **Create Account**.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email" icon="mail">

    Check your inbox for a verification email from `no-reply@petsgram.net`.

    Click the **Verify Email** button in the email.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Setup" icon="check-circle">

    Return to Petsgram and log in. Your account is now active!

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Post Your First Pet Content

Share a photo or video of your pet using the web or app.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web" icon="globe">

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Upload Media" icon="upload">

        Click **Post** on the dashboard.

        Drag and drop your pet photo or video (max 50MB).

      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Caption" icon="edit-3">

        Write a caption like "My dog Max at the park!"

        Add hashtags: `#Petsgram #DogLovers #MaxTheDog`

      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish" icon="send">

        Click **Share** to post publicly.

      </Step>
    </Steps>

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">

    Download the Petsgram app from App Store or Google Play.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Tap +" icon="plus">

        Tap the `+` icon at the bottom.

      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Media" icon="image">

        Choose from camera roll or take new photo/video.

      </Step>
      <Step title="Tag and Post" icon="hash">

        Add `#Petsgram` and publish.

      </Step>
    </Steps>

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">

Use `#Petsgram` in every post to feature it on the main page. Combine with breed-specific tags like `#GoldenRetriever` for more visibility.

</Callout>

## Maximize Visibility with Hashtags

Hashtags connect your content to the community.

```javascript
// Example: Generate a shareable post URL with hashtags
const postUrl = "https://petsgram.net/posts/your-post-id";
const hashtags = "#Petsgram,#PetLovers,#CutePets";
const shareLink = `${postUrl}?hashtags=${encodeURIComponent(hashtags)}`;
console.log(shareLink);
```

```bash
# Share via command line (for automation)
curl -X POST https://petsgram.net/api/share \
  -d 'postId=123&hashtags="#Petsgram #CatOfInstagram"'
```

## Overview

Petsgram connects pet owners worldwide through a vibrant social network where you share photos and stories of your furry friends. Use the special `#petsgram` hashtag to feature your posts on the main feed, building a community around adorable pet moments. You also access an integrated online store stocked with premium pet products, from toys to treats.

Our mission empowers pet lovers to showcase their companions, foster connections, and discover essential gear—all in one platform.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes with these steps.

<Steps>

<Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">

Visit `https://petsgram.net` and sign up with your email or social login. Verify your account to unlock posting.

</Step>

<Step title="Share Your First Post" icon="camera">

Upload a pet photo, add caption and `#petsgram` hashtag. Hit publish to enter the featured feed.

````javascript
// Example post via API
const postData = {
  image: 'https://your-pet-photo.jpg',
  caption: 'Meet my dog Max! #petsgram',
  tags: ['dog', 'petsgram']
};

fetch('https://api.petsgram.net/posts', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer ${YOUR_TOKEN}` },
  body: JSON.stringify(postData)
});
````

</Step>

<Step title="Explore Store" icon="shopping-bag">

Navigate to the store tab. Add items to cart and checkout using saved payment methods.

</Step>

</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

Pro tip: Complete your profile with pet details to attract more followers and boost your featured chances.

</Callout>

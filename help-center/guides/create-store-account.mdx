## Shop for Your Pet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the store">
    ````
    Visit https://petsgram.net/store
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign up or log in">
    ````
    Click "Sign Up" and enter your details.
    Or log in if you already have a Petsgram account.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start shopping">
    ````
    Browse products and add items to your cart.
    Proceed to checkout when ready.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>

description: Fix upload issues on Petsgram.net.
---

## Problem

- Photo upload stuck or fails.
- Error message: "Upload failed."

## Quick Fixes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check file size">
    ````
    Photos must be under 10MB and in JPG/PNG.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your connection">
    ````
    Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try on another device">
    ````
    Attempt upload from a different browser or phone.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>

Still stuck? Email support@petsgram.net
